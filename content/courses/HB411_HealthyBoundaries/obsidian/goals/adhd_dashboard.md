## ğŸš¨ Trigger Identification

### Known Focus Killers

1. **Social Media**: Especially in low-energy states
2. **Open Loops**: Unfinished tasks create anxiety
3. **Ambiguous Tasks**: Need clear next actions
4. **Emotional Dysregulation**: Rejection, frustration
5. **Physical**: Hunger, dehydration, poor sleep

### Warning Signs

- Checking phone repeatedly
- Task switching every few minutes
- Doom scrolling
- Irritability spike
- "Just checking" behaviors

### Intervention Protocols

**When noticing warning signs:**

1. STOP - Name what's happening
2. STEP BACK - 5 deep breaths
3. OBSERVE - What triggered this?
4. PROCEED - Choose one grounding action

**Emergency reset options:**

- [ ] 10-minute walk
- [ ] Cold water on face
- [ ] Quick meditation
- [ ] Call accountability partner
- [ ] Change environment
- [ ] Eat/drink something
