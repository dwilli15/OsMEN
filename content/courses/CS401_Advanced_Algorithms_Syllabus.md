### Course Description
This advanced course covers algorithmic techniques for solving complex computational problems including dynamic programming, graph algorithms, NP-completeness, and approximation algorithms.

### Prerequisites
- CS 201: Data Structures
- CS 250: Discrete Mathematics
- MATH 301: Linear Algebra

### Required Textbook
- "Introduction to Algorithms" by Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, and Stein (4th Edition)
