Google Calendar

Google Calendar requires OAuth setup. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to Google Cloud Console
  2. Create OAuth 2.0 credentials
  3. Download credentials.json
  4. Set GOOGLE_CREDENTIALS_PATH env variable

Getting Started

To sync your syllabus events with your calendar:

  1. Connect at least one calendar provider above
  2. Go to the Syllabus Parser page
  3. Upload your syllabus PDF or DOCX
  4. Review and select the events you want to add
  5. Click "Add to Calendar" to sync

Your calendar credentials are stored locally and never sent to external servers.
